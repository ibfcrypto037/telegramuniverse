What is a Good CPM Rate for Telegram Channels?

By TelegramUniverse Team • 6 min read

When you start monetizing your channel, the first confusing metric you will encounter is CPM, or Cost Per Mille. It represents the cost an advertiser pays for 1,000 views of their advertisement. Consequently, your telegram ads revenue per 1000 views heavily depends on your audience's origin, purchasing power, and channel niche. We are diving deep to uncover how much does telegram pay per view and what a "good" CPM legitimately looks like.

Telegram CPM Rates by Country Tiers

Not all views are created equal in the eyes of an advertiser. Because buying power heavily fluctuates across the globe, marketers organize countries into tiers. Here is a breakdown of average telegram cpm rates by country.

Tier 1: High Purchasing Power

Comprising countries like the United States, United Kingdom, Canada, Australia, and parts of the EU (Germany, France). CPMs here are astronomical compared to the rest of the world. A native organic sponsorship in a Tier 1 channel can effortlessly command $10 to $30+ CPM based on the niche (even higher for finance/crypto). The best country audience for telegram revenue is undeniably a Tier 1 demographic.

Tier 2: Developing Economies

Countries such as Russia, Brazil, Turkey, and growing markets in Eastern Europe. The Telegram penetration in these regions is huge, meaning there's massive liquidity but slightly lower advertising spend. CPMs typically hang out in the $2 to $8 CPM range. It’s highly profitable purely due to the easily attainable volume of subscribers.

Tier 3: Emerging Markets

India, parts of Southeast Asia, and Africa. Because consumer ad spend is significantly lower here, the CPM range generally sits between $0.20 and $1.50 CPM. To profit heavily here requires massive multi-million view networks.

Niche Matters Just as Much

Geography is only half the battle. Advertisers evaluate the topic of your channel to gauge the relevance of your followers:

  • Crypto & Finance: $15 - $40+ CPMs. Easily the most lucrative.
  • Tech & Business: $8 - $20 CPMs. Very solid, reliable earners.
  • Entertainment & Memes: $1 - $4 CPMs. Fun content, but extremely difficult to convert to sales for generic advertisers.
