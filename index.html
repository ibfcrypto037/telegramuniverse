FAQ

What is Telegram revenue?
Telegram displays sponsored messages in large public channels. Channel owners earn revenue by displaying these ads, sharing profits with Telegram.
How does 50/50 split work?
Telegram keeps 50% of the ad revenue generated on your channel, and the creator gets the remaining 50%. Our tools calculate your true cut automatically.
Why tier matters?
Advertisers pay more to show ads to users in Tier 1 countries (like USA, UK, Germany) compared to other regions, resulting in a higher CPM for your channel.
Is this accurate?
These are estimates based on market averages and current CPM trends. Actual earnings will vary based on your channel's specific niche and audience behavior.
How to increase revenue?
Focus on improving user engagement, growing real subscribers, and targeting audiences in higher-tier countries to attract better advertiser rates.
