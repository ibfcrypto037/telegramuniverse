Contrast this strict baseline heavily with Telegram's new offering. Telegram now distributes a massive, completely flat 50% cut of their highly native advertising platform revenue. That superficially might appear perfectly balanced and similar mathematically on a spreadsheet.

However, the actual total bankable telegram vs youtube earnings breakdown varies completely differently. YouTube videos naturally stack ads before, during, and directly after a video, maximizing impressions over weeks and years. Telegram focuses heavily on high impression density immediately on a mobile locked screen over just twenty-four hours.

The Crushing Weight of Production Execution

The sheer paralyzing overhead of efficiently managing a highly successful YouTube channel is fundamentally tremendous. Six-figure earning YouTubers absolutely must dedicate exhaustive hours to script writing, dynamic pacing, timeline editing, and aggressively split-testing visually flawless clickbait thumbnails.
